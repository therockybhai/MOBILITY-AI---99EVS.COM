# 99EVS Predictive Risk Forecast Layer (PRFL)

Purpose:

Forecast upcoming vehicle reliability risks using lifecycle intelligence trends.

Outputs:

Battery Replacement Forecast

Mechanical Service Risk Forecast

Confidence Decay Projection

Resale Timing Recommendation

---

## Forecast Inputs

Battery Risk Index trend

Mechanical Reliability Index trend

Trust Score evolution trend

Inspection frequency timeline

vehicle age factor

usage category signal
---

## Battery Replacement Forecast Model

Inputs:

BRI trend slope

charging response drift

thermal stability change

cycle-age estimate progression

Outputs:

replacement unlikely within 12 months

replacement possible within 6–12 months

replacement likely within 3–6 months

replacement imminent
---

## Mechanical Service Risk Forecast Model

Inputs:

MRI degradation slope

brake wear progression

suspension drift variance

controller response stability

Outputs:

service not expected soon

service likely within 6–12 months

service likely within 3–6 months

service recommended immediately
---

## Confidence Decay Projection Model

Inputs:

Trust Score change velocity

inspection integrity variance

fraud risk fluctuation

inspection completeness trend

Outputs:

stable confidence projection

slow decay projection

moderate decay projection

rapid decay projection
---

## Resale Timing Recommendation Model

Inputs:

battery forecast

mechanical forecast

confidence decay projection

market stability score

Outputs:

optimal resale window

recommended resale soon

hold vehicle

sell immediately
---

## Event-Based Prediction Signals

Predictive risk models include:

fault frequency

service interval variance

## Telemetry-Based Prediction Signals
