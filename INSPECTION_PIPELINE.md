# 99EVS Inspection Intelligence Pipeline

Objective:

Improve vehicle inspection scoring reliability and automation readiness.

Tasks:

1. Validate inspection completeness
2. Detect missing inspection inputs
3. Prepare damage-weight scoring structure
4. Prepare battery health risk indicators
5. Suggest valuation confidence score

Priority modules:

battery condition
controller response
motor noise
chassis damage
cosmetic status
accessory completeness

Outputs:

inspection score
risk score
valuation confidence %
