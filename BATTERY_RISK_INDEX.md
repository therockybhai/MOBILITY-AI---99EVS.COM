# 99EVS Battery Risk Index (BRI)

Purpose:

Estimate probability of battery-related resale failure risk.

Applicable Vehicle Types:

electric 2W
electric 3W
electric 4W
hybrid vehicles
future energy storage platforms

Output:

Battery Risk Score
Battery Confidence %
Replacement Risk Category
---

## Battery Risk Inputs

charge retention

voltage stability

charging response

cycle age estimate
---

## Vehicle Type Weight Adjustment

2-wheelers:

charge retention 40%
voltage stability 20%
thermal behavior 10%
cycle age estimate 20%
BMS status 10%

3-wheelers:

charge retention 35%
voltage stability 25%
cycle age estimate 20%
thermal behavior 10%
connector condition 10%

4-wheelers:

BMS diagnostics 30%
voltage stability 25%
thermal behavior 15%
cycle age estimate 20%
connector condition 10%
---
