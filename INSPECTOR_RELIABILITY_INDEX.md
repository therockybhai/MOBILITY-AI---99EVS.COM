# 99EVS Inspector Reliability Index (IRI)

Purpose:

Evaluate inspection quality reliability across inspectors.

Outputs:

Inspector Reliability Score

Inspector Trust Tier

Inspection Confidence Multiplier
---

## Inspector Reliability Inputs

inspection completeness consistency

inspection error rate

certificate revision frequency

partner feedback score
---

## Inspector Reliability Weight Distribution

inspection completeness consistency = 25%

reinspection variance deviation = 25%

partner feedback score = 20%

certificate revision frequency = 15%

inspection turnaround time = 15%
---
