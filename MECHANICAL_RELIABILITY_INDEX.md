# 99EVS Mechanical Reliability Index (MRI)

Purpose:

Estimate driveline and control-system reliability risk affecting resale confidence.

Applicable Vehicle Types:

electric 2W
electric 3W
electric 4W
ICE vehicles
hybrid vehicles
future industrial machines

Output:

Mechanical Reliability Score
Mechanical Confidence %
Repair Risk Category
---

## Mechanical Risk Inputs

motor condition

controller response

brake system health

suspension condition

steering alignment

wiring harness condition

noise / vibration behavior
---

## Powertrain-Specific Inputs

Electric Vehicles:

motor torque response

controller fault signals

regen braking response

throttle smoothness


ICE Vehicles:

engine noise stability

gearbox response

clutch behavior

exhaust smoke condition

---

## Mechanical Risk Categories

90–100%

Very Low Risk
Vehicle mechanically stable


75–89%

Low Risk
Minor wear expected


60–74%

Moderate Risk
Service intervention likely


Below 60%

High Risk
Repair required soon
