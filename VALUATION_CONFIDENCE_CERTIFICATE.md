# 99EVS Valuation Confidence Certificate (VCC)

Purpose:

Generate structured valuation confidence report for inspected vehicles.

Outputs:

Confidence Score

Battery Risk Index

Mechanical Reliability Index

Inspection Integrity Status

Adjusted Valuation Range

Risk Classification Summary
---

## Certificate Sections

Vehicle identity summary

Inspection completeness score

Battery Risk Index

Mechanical Reliability Index

Cosmetic condition score

Accessory completeness score

Market stability score

Final valuation confidence %

Adjusted resale value band
---

## Certificate Reliability Bands

90–100%

Certified High Confidence Vehicle


75–89%

Certified Reliable Vehicle


60–74%

Moderate Confidence Vehicle


Below 60%

Inspection Advisory Required
---

## Confidence Disclaimer Logic

Confidence score reflects inspection-based predictive reliability only.

Actual resale value may vary based on:

market timing

buyer demand

battery usage variation

location-specific liquidity
---
