Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

99EVS.com is operated by Kiran SR. Contact: 99evsworld@gmail.com

What We Collect

  • Account data: Name and email on registration.
  • Vehicle data: Vehicle details you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage data: Questions you ask MobilityAI, to improve the platform.
  • Google data: Name, email, profile picture if you sign in with Google.

What We Never Do

  • We never sell your data to any third party.
  • We never show ads based on your conversations.
  • We never share personal information without consent.
  • We never store your password in plain text.

How We Protect Your Data

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are stored only in Vercel's encrypted environment variables — never in source code. Server-side authentication means your credentials never appear in browser code.

AI Conversations

Your conversations with MobilityAI are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy for details.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact 99evsworld@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies. No advertising cookies.
