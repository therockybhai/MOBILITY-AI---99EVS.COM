Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

99EVS.com is a mobility intelligence platform operated by Kiran SR. We provide AI-powered vehicle intelligence through MobilityAI. Contact: 99evsworld@gmail.com

What Data We Collect

  • Account data: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Vehicle data: Vehicle details you voluntarily provide to MobilityAI for personalised answers.
  • Usage data: Questions you ask MobilityAI. We use this to improve the platform.
  • Google data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email and profile picture from Google.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We never sell your data to any third party.
  • We never show ads based on your conversations or vehicle data.
  • We never share your personal information without your explicit consent.
  • We never store your password in plain text — passwords are hashed and stored securely.

How We Protect Your Data

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored in source code — they are kept in secure environment variables on Vercel's encrypted infrastructure. We use server-side authentication so your credentials never appear in browser code.

AI Conversations

Your conversations with MobilityAI are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy for details on how they handle conversation data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at 99evsworld@gmail.com to exercise these rights. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.
