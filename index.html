Important Safety Disclaimer

MobilityAI provides educational insights and diagnostic guidance. It does not replace professional mechanical inspection or manufacturer service recommendations. Diagnostic outputs are AI-assisted analysis based on provided information — they are not definitive mechanical assessments. Never attempt repairs beyond your skill level based solely on AI guidance.
